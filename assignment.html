Assignment

I have watched the movie The Shawshank Redemption and below are my answers to the questions:

Questions:

  1. Your favourite dialogue and why?
  2. Your personal Review.
  3. Learning to take away from the movie.
  4. Related concepts you've learned in the classroom.

Answers:

1) Your favourite dialogue and why?
My favorite dialogue from the movie is:

"Get busy living, or get busy dying."

This line resonates deeply because it encapsulates the essence of choice and hope.

2) Your personal Review:
The Shawshank Redemption is an extraordinary film that masterfully blends themes of hope, resilience, and friendship.

3) Learning to take away from the movie:
The key takeaway is the transformative power of hope.

4) Related concepts you've learned in the classroom:
Resilience and Coping Mechanisms: How individuals develop resilience in adversity.
Institutional Behavior: How institutions shape or constrain behavior.
The Power of Narrative: Storytelling as a tool for change.
